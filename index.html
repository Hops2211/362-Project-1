Park Community Church Children's Ministry

Volunteer Ministry

Here at Park we are a church that relies on and thrives on the work of our amazing volunteers. They Help us to create an enviroment where the parents can focus on worshipping and receiving God's word while trusting that their children are in good hands. In our children's ministry (The Loop), we have variety of classes for children of different age groups.

  1. Blue Line - Ages: Birth to 1 year
  2. Brown Line - Ages: 1 years to 2 years
  3. Green Line - Ages: 2 years- 5 years
  4. Purple Line - Ages: 5 years - 7 years
  5. Red Line - Ages: 7 years - 10 years

We welcome everyone from all backgrounds, and of all talents. If you have an interest in serving our ministry and an interest in raising up our children to walk in God's footsteps then we have an interest in you.
